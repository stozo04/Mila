My Precious Mila,
Novemeber 30, 2024

Thanksgiving, Tiny Steps, and Big Discoveries

Every month with you feels like an adventure, but this one was particularly heartwarming. Watching you grow and blossom into your own little personality is such a joy, and this month was no exception. From family-filled holidays to those tiny but oh-so-precious milestones, you continue to amaze us every day.

Growing Up So Fast 🌙

One of the sweetest new things you’ve started doing is telling us when you’re ready for "night night." When we see you start rubbing your eyes, Mama and I will ask you if you are ready for night night. Sometimes you agree, and sometimes you say no—especially when you’re not quite done playing with your Lala (baby). It’s so adorable how you’re beginning to understand us and respond with a little “yes” or “no.” Watching you grow and communicate this way fills our hearts with so much joy.

“Papa Poo Poo” 🚽

Another big milestone this month: you’ve started to tell us when you “poo poo.” Sometimes you’ll announce it with a giggle, saying, “Papa poo poo!” It’s the cutest thing, even when we don’t quite make it to the bathroom on time. Occasionally, you turn it into a little game, running off and laughing as we chase after you. It’s moments like these that remind me to embrace the chaos and celebrate the progress, even when it’s messy!

Running Into the Future 🏃‍♀️✨

Mila, you are officially trying to run now! I remember thinking you’d never start walking, and now here you are, zooming around with all the energy in the world. You stumble sometimes, but that doesn’t stop you—you’re determined to go faster, to explore more, to chase after life with those tiny legs of yours. Watching you run is like seeing your spirit in action: bold, curious, and unafraid.

Little Hands, Big Interests 🌟

You’ve become so curious about everything lately, and I can’t get enough of it. Whether it’s playing in the sand, helping Mama cook, or feeding the animals, you throw yourself into every new experience with so much enthusiasm. You even help throw away your diaper now, a tiny act that feels like a big leap toward growing up.

Thanksgiving in Houston 🦃💛

We ended the month with a trip to Houston to celebrate Thanksgiving. It was a special time filled with love, laughter, and the warmth of family. You were the center of attention, of course—your cousins and relatives couldn’t get enough of you! Seeing you run around, giggling and exploring, reminded us how much joy you bring to everyone you meet. It’s moments like these that remind us how lucky we are to have such a sweet little light in our lives.

Clean Hands, Full Heart 🧼🖐️❤️

One of my favorite things is your meticulous little habit of wanting clean hands. Every time you eat, you stop to hold up your sticky fingers, demanding they be wiped clean before you take another bite. It’s adorable and oh-so-you. I have to admit, you get that from me—I don’t like dirty hands either! It’s these quirks that make you uniquely you, and I wouldn’t change a thing.

Mila, as you grow, I find myself marveling at the incredible little person you’re becoming. Each month, you teach us so much about love, patience, and the joy of discovery. Keep running, exploring, and being your wonderful self. We’ll always be here to guide you, cheer you on, and celebrate every step of the way. We love you more than words can say. ❤️

With all the love in the world,

Your Papa
