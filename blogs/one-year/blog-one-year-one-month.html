My Precious Mila,
June 30, 2024

Today you turned 1 Year 1 Month (13 Months).

This month has been a whirlwind of celebration and firsts as you marked a truly special milestone—your first birthday! We traveled to Florida to celebrate this grand occasion, surrounded by all your loving family. The joy and warmth of gathering together for your big day made it an unforgettable experience.

During our stay, you experienced the ocean for the first time. Watching your tiny toes sink into the warm sand and the gentle waves brushing against your feet was a truly heartwarming sight. Initially, the waves startled you, and you clung to us in apprehension. But gradually, with the reassurance of either mom's or dad's embrace, you began to enjoy the water around your legs.

Another first was your ride on the ferris wheel, where we soared above the beach, taking in views from a dizzying height. Your fascination with the vast, sprawling ocean from up high mirrored our own awe not just at the scene, but at the journey we’ve embarked on with you this past year.

While you haven’t started walking on your own just yet, you’ve made delightful progress; you love walking while clutching our hands. Each tentative step you take, guided by our fingers, is not just about balance and movement, but about trust and connection. It’s incredibly sweet and a beautiful reminder of how you’re growing and learning each day.

Mila, every moment with you is a chapter filled with love and discovery. Your first year has flown by, and yet each day with you is a vivid imprint in our lives, a beautiful blend of the past’s cherished memories and the future’s endless possibilities. Here's to many more years of joy, growth, and the simple, profound pleasure of walking hand in hand with you.

With all the love in the world,

Your Papa
