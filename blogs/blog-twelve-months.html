My Precious Mila,
May 30, 2024

Today you turned 12 months old.
  19 lbs .5 oz
  28.5 Inches

As I sit down to write these words on your first birthday, I am overwhelmed by the love and joy you have brought into my life. It feels like just yesterday I held you for the first time, and now you are already one year old. This year has been filled with so many precious moments, each one more beautiful than the last.

Today is such a bittersweet day. You turned one year old, and I am filled with joy and pride as I watch you grow. At the same time, your pawpaw is undergoing open heart surgery. It's funny how history and this world work because when I was born, your meme (pawpaw's mom) was also having open heart surgery. This is a tradition that I am not fond of, but it reminds me of the strength and resilience that runs in our family.

From the moment you entered this world, you have painted my life with colors I never knew existed. Your laughter is the most beautiful melody, and your smile brightens even the darkest days. Watching you grow and discover the world has been a privilege beyond words. Your tiny fingers that once clung to mine with such trust are now reaching out to explore everything around you with curiosity and wonder.

Seeing bits of myself and your mom in you fills me with awe and pride. You are our most precious gift, and every little milestone you've reached has been a cause for celebration. Your first giggle, your first crawl, and now your first wobbly steps are memories I will cherish forever. You have taught me to see the world anew, to appreciate the small wonders and simple joys of life.

My Precious Mila, I promise to be there for you every step of the way as you continue to grow. I will be your guide, your protector, and your biggest cheerleader. Together, we will explore, learn, and face life's challenges hand in hand. Though I may not have all the answers, I will always strive to help you find your way with love and wisdom.

As your father, my love for you knows no bounds. I am committed to creating a world for you where you feel cherished, empowered, and free to chase your dreams. In your eyes, I see a future filled with endless possibilities, and I am so excited to witness the incredible person you are destined to become.

Being your dad is the greatest privilege of my life, and as I write this letter, I am filled with gratitude and anticipation for the journey ahead. Happy first birthday, my sweet Mila. Here's to many more years of love, laughter, and unforgettable memories.

With all the love in the world,

Your Papa
