Today you turned one year old. I can’t believe how fast time has flown by and what a year it has been.

You are my cutie patootie. Since your arrival, you have been nothing but a blessing in our lives. Your little T-Rex cooing noises have turned into cute little giggles. Your little helpless body is turning into a fearless toddler. You are growing so fast, and it is amazing to watch all the new things you are discovering.

We are growing beside you with your papa. We don’t know everything, but we do know that we want to give you only the best. The world is yours, and nothing can stop you. You just have to reach for it, and we will be right there supporting you.

I love you with all my heart, my sweet girl. Never change; be the person you are meant to be.
