My Precious Mila,
January 30, 2024

Today you turned 8 months old.
  16 lbs 7 oz
  TODO inches

The highlight of the month was undoubtedly witnessing your first tooth. A tiny, pearly white on the bottom of your gum started to make its appearance. Your mom and I are beyond excited about this development, eagerly anticipating the many toothy grins that await us.

Your first encounter with snow was nothing short of magical. We were visiting Baba's house when the world outside transformed into a winter wonderland. Wrapped up in your warmest clothes, we introduced you to the snow, watching as your tiny hands reached out to touch the cold, fluffy white for the first time. Your wide-eyed wonder were pure joy to witness, making it a moment we'll cherish forever.

However, the snow-filled weekend took an unexpected turn when you caught the flu, leading to a hospital visit with a worrying temperature of 104 degrees. That week, both your mom and I also succumbed to the flu, making it a particularly challenging time for our little family. Despite the rough days, the strength and resilience you showed were inspiring, reminding us of the preciousness of health and family.

Amidst these ups and downs, you achieved another remarkable milestone – learning to hold your bottle as you drink. Watching you grasp the bottle with your tiny hands, determined and focused, filled us with pride. It's a significant step towards independence, and you're handling it with the same eagerness you've shown towards all your milestones.

This month also saw you starting to eat more whole foods, including fish, pasta, and green beans. Each new flavor and texture you experience is a delight to watch, as you navigate the complexities of taste and preference. It's a joy to see you embracing these new foods, expanding your palate with every meal.

Mila, each day with you is a gift, filled with new challenges, achievements, and countless moments of joy. As we navigate the highs and lows together, we're reminded of the incredible journey of growth and discovery we're on with you. Here's to the many more milestones to come, and the endless memories we'll create together.

With all the love in the world,

Your Papa
