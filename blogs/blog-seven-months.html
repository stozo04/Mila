My Precious Mila,
December 30, 2023

Today you turned 7 months old.
  16 lbs 1 oz
  TODO inches

This month has been a whirlwind of firsts and festive celebrations. Your journey through these new experiences has filled our hearts with immeasurable joy and pride.

Your first Thanksgiving and Christmas in Houston were nothing short of magical. These celebrations were a tapestry of love, laughter, and family traditions, now even more special with your presence. Aunt SheShe's gift, a cute shirt with "Resting Grinch Face" just for you, and her matching one, "The Original Resting Grinch Face," added a playful touch to our holiday. The warmth of the holiday spirit embraced us as we gathered around, thankful for the gift of you in our lives. Your inclusion in our annual family photos is a memory I will always hold dear, your tiny presence adding immeasurable joy to these cherished snapshots. Watching you open presents, your little hands exploring with awe, was truly beautiful, each unwrapping a moment of pure wonder. Your eyes sparkled with excitement at the sights and sounds of the season, adding a new layer of excitement to our festivities.

Then came your first Russian Christmas in Dallas, a heartfelt celebration of your mother's culture and traditions. It was a deeply meaningful experience for me to introduce you to these customs, a part of your heritage that I am eager to pass on to you. Celebrating the Russian Christmas with your mother's family has always been among my most treasured memories, and I hope that, as you grow, these moments become just as special for you. Your interactions with cousins Liza and Bella, who took such joy in babysitting and playing with you, added a wonderful sense of family and connection to our festivities. Watching you immerse in these experiences was a beautiful reminder of the rich cultural tapestry you are a part of, a legacy that I hope you will cherish and embrace with each passing year.

Amidst these celebrations, your physical milestones have been equally awe-inspiring. You are mastering the art of army crawling, moving with determination and curiosity. Each little push forward marks your growing independence and eagerness to explore the world around you.

Your ability to sit up on your own is another remarkable achievement. It's a joy to see you observing your surroundings with such focus and delight, sitting upright like a little explorer ready for her next adventure.

One of the most heartwarming highlights this month was your reunion with your best friend Iza. The bond you share is a beautiful thing to witness. And let's not forget the adorable outfits Mayumi crafted for you both for Thanksgiving and Christmas. These outfits were not just garments but symbols of love and the festive spirit, making you and Iza the stars of the holiday season.

Each day with you, Mila, is a story filled with love, growth, and discovery. As you continue to reach new milestones and forge precious relationships, know that you are the heart of our family's joy. Here's to more firsts, more celebrations, and a lifetime of sweet memories together.

With all the love in the world,

Your Papa
