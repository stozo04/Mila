August 17, 2023

My Precious Mila,

Each day with you feels like gift and watching you grow, learn, and evolve leaves me in awe. Such a big milestone was reached last night! You slept throughout the night, from 9 pm to 6:30 am! My heart swells with pride as I think of how beautifully you're growing. Every new achievement of yours, no matter how small, feels monumental.

Speaking of heartwarming moments, witnessing your newfound fascination with your arms is a sheer delight. Its the cutest thing to watch. (I tell mama that it is better then TV). As you lay in your bed, lifting your tiny arms and examining them with an innocent wonder. Sometimes, I find myself getting lost in those precious moments, watching you remain engrossed in your little discovery sessions. Thirty minutes might seem long to others, but for me, it's a timeless journey of watching you connect with parts of yourself, laying the foundation for many more discoveries to come.

Every day brings its own tale of growth and adventure, and I am infinitely grateful to be a part of your incredible journey. I can't wait to see what other surprises you have in store for me.
